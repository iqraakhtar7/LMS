Annual Dinner is a legacy; an official, annual event hosted by DCS-UBIT, University of Karachi for the purpose of building a strong connection of students, teachers and alumni of the department. It is the only official event of UBIT where all of the faculty members are invited. Students are benefited by teachers' advice and enlightenment about the tech industry they are about to jump in by indulging themselves in different informal interactive sessions or one on one conversations with the respected teachers and alumni.
